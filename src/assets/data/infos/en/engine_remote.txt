 You can operate it as a PPT screen on the web and as a PPT controller on the app.

 You can get the basic form of PPT operated by engine at https://github.com/is2you2/engineWorksPPT.git. If you are using EnginePPT.tscn as the main scene, you can start with a new project.
 Since PPT works on the web, review is required if it contains 3D elements that may be restricted on the web.

 Your phone and computer must be on the same network.
